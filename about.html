About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence in Mukurwe-ini.

Who We Are

An Institution of Choice

Located in the heart of Gikondi Ward, Mukurwe-ini sub County, Nyeri County Karaba Mixed Secondary School is dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals. We pride ourselves on a serene learning environment that balances academic rigor with co-curricular activities.

Our Roots

Brief History

Karaba Mixed Secondary School was founded on 2008 serve the Mukurwe-ini community by providing affordable, high-quality education. From humble beginnings, we have grown in Academics and co-curricular excellence.

2008

Foundation

The school opened its doors with only 40 students and 3 teachers.

2012

First Science Lab

Commissioning of the first Laboratory block by the area MP.

2024

Launch of the state-of-the-art computer lab and library extension.
